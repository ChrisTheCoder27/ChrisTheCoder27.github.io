## Call of Doodles: Stickmen

- Group project <br>
- Estimated time: 5 months <br>
- Game engine: Unity <br>
<br>

Call of Doodles: Stickmen is a wave-based FPS game where you fight off hordes of paper stickmen using origami weapons. I worked on this game with a team of 5 other people for my 2nd year of university in game development. I primarily worked on programming the weapons for the game, the player movement, as well as the enemies, including their AI behaviours and spawning systems. I also implemented the logic for the powerups that randomly spawn in, the gameplay and menu UIs, and the visual effect that happens when you defeat an enemy.
